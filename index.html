Our Story

The Place Where Sheridan Gets Sharp

The Office Barbershop is Sheridan, Wyoming's go-to destination for precision grooming, honest prices, and a chair that always feels like it was waiting for you. We don't rush. We don't cut corners — only hair.

Our barbers combine time-honored techniques with a modern understanding of what today's client wants. Whether you're coming in for a classic hot shave, a clean fade, or bringing in the kids — every visit gets the same attention, craft, and care.

We believe great grooming should be accessible to everyone. Our shop is fully wheelchair accessible, family-friendly, and staffed by people who genuinely enjoy their work and their community.
